Overview

Built for factories that need stable VOC treatment.

The RTO system uses regenerative ceramic heat storage and high-temperature oxidation to decompose VOCs into CO₂ and H₂O. It is suitable for continuous industrial exhaust streams where stable operation, heat recovery and emission compliance are key requirements.

01

High VOC Removal

Designed for efficient thermal oxidation of organic waste gases.

02

Heat Recovery

Regenerative ceramic beds help reduce operating energy demand.

03

System Integration

Can be combined with filters, scrubbers, ESP or rotor systems.

04

Stable Operation

Suitable for continuous industrial production lines.

Process Flow

How the RTO system works.

1

Pre-treatment

Dust, sticky mist or moisture are reduced to protect the RTO core.

2

Heat Storage

Exhaust gas passes through ceramic media and absorbs stored heat.

3

Oxidation

VOCs are decomposed at high temperature in the oxidation chamber.

4

Heat Recovery

Clean hot gas transfers heat back to the ceramic bed for reuse.

5

Discharge

Treated gas is discharged after meeting the designed emission target.

Common ConfigurationPre-filter / scrubber / ESP + RTO main unit + induced draft fan + stack + control cabinet.
