Core Route 02

Route Logic

Oil mist, smoke and odor need staged treatment.

Why not direct carbon?

Oil mist, sticky particles and high humidity can shorten carbon life. Pre-treatment protects adsorption and improves stability.

Why add ESP?

ESP captures fine oil mist and smoke that are difficult for normal filtration, especially in pelletizing and rubber exhaust.

Why use CO or RTO?

Oxidation is selected when VOC load or emission target requires destruction rather than only adsorption.
